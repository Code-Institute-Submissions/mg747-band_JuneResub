Early Life and Career Beginnings:

Def Bond is an Irish Alternative Hip-Hop group founded in Dublin 2008, members are producer and rapper Tango (born Brian Knox) and chrone vocalist Kash (born Karma Don). The two members were childhood friends raised in Malahide, suburban Dublin. They started skateboarding and participating in street battle rap at a very young age. Shortly after the duo had developed their skills and well known nationally, they were invited to rap live on record producer and music executive DJ Blink radio show. DJ Blink signed them to his Compass Record Label. Shortly after they moved to California. In 2014, Tango mentioned in a interview with Complex Magazine that the first two years of their career was the toughest, because fame had lead them to fast life in Los Angeles, that almost affected our creative process of making quality music.

MUSIC

Def Bond released their first full-lenght album "FADED" in April 2010. It included colaboration with artists like Big Louch, Glazer Boiz and Chanel Seven. It debuted at No. 3 at the Billboard Hot 100, and sold over 360,000 copies in its first week. The album went platinum in a matter of weeks, and recieved a Fanny nomination, and also spawned hits such as "Daytona," "Levitate," and "Break Free." In 2011, Def Bond followed up the success of their first album with WILD WEST, which was massively successful, debuting at No. 1 on the Billboard charts, selling 410,000 in its first week, and has gone double platinum since its release. The Album also won them their first Fanny Award for rap album of the year. Three years elapsed between the release of WILD WEST and their third studio album ANTHEM but they continued to collaborate with other artists and formed their own record label, TK Entertainment, which is currently distributed by Global Records. When Anthem was released in september 2014, It went No. 1 on the Billboard charts making it their second No. 1 album, and sold 470,000 copies in its first week. The album has gone platinum ever since.

PHILANTROPHY

Def Bond is active in giving back, most notably through their philantropic work through the Who We Be Foundation. The foundation, founded in 2016, is dedicated to helping individuals facing social-economical hardship further their education, and encouraging them to tap into their potential.

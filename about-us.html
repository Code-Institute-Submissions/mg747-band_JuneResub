ABOUT US

Early Life and Career Beginnings

Already distinctive power on their own, the combination of Morgan G (MG) and Kamron Don (kash) formed an Irish Hip-Hop group later to be known as Da Lick in Dublin 2008. Rapper, songwriter and producer MG and singer and multi-instrumentalist Kash, were childhood friends raised in Malahide; suburban Dublin. They started skateboarding and participating in street rap battle at a very young age. Shortly after the duo had developed their skills and well known nationally, they were invited to rap live on record producer and music executive DJ Bling radio show. DJ Bling signed them to his Mega Jam Record Label. In 2009 they relocated to California to record their debut album. In 2014, MG mentioned in an interview with Complex Magazine that moving to Los Angeles has helped their career in a major way. The love and support from fans all over the world is what we live for. We just want to continue to make quality music for our fans.

Music

Da Lick released their debut album Exodus in July 7 2009. It included colaboration with artists like Def Bond, Chanel Seven and Big Louch. It debuted at No. 3 at the Billboard Hot 100, and sold over 160,000 copies in its first week. The album went platinum in a matter of weeks, and recieved a Grammy nomination, and also spawned hits such as "Daytona," "Exodus," and "Break Free." In 2011, they followed up the success of their first album with WILD WEST, which was massively successful, debuting at No. 1 on the Billboard charts, selling 210,000 in its first week, and has gone double platinum since its release. The Album also won them their first Grammy Award for rap album of the year. Three years elapsed between the release of WILD WEST and their third studio album PTSD but they continued to collaborate with other artists and formed their own record label, HITEKH Records, which is currently distributed by Global Grind Entertainment. When PTSD was released in September 2014, It went No. 1 on the Billboard charts making it their second No. 1 album, and sold 370,000 copies in its first week. The album has gone platinum ever since. Their last studio album SYMPHONY, was released in march 12 2021.

Philantrophy

Da Lick is active in giving back, most notably through their philantropic work through the Gogetter Foundation. The foundation, founded in 2016, is dedicated to helping individuals facing social-economical hardship further their education, and encouraging them to tap into their potential.
